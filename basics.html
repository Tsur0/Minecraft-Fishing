The Basics of Fishing

Rudimentary!

Fishing in Minecraft requires only three things:

  • Fishing Rod
  • Patience
  • Water

You cast your fishing rod into the water, and wait until the bobber dips down, then reel in and receive your catch!

Fishing in Minecraft can give some wealth however. There are three categories of catches.

  • Fish (Obviously)
  • Junk Items
  • Treasure

Junk items give you purposely useless items. Treasure items give you Enchanted items. Whether a Fishing Rod or a Bow that is already enchanted, or even a book that's enchanted; which you can use to enchant any item of your choosing.
